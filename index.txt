You arrive in a city with n sights, labeled 0..n-1. Sight 0 is your hotel.
Each sight i has a beauty value beauty[i].

There are m bidirectional roads. Road k connects u[k] and v[k] and takes t[k] minutes to travel.
You may travel along roads multiple times.

You have at most max_t minutes total travel time. You must start at sight 0 and finish at sight 0.

When you visit a sight, you collect its beauty value only the FIRST time you visit that sight
(revisits do not add extra beauty).

Task:
Determine the maximum total beauty you can collect within max_t minutes while starting and ending at 0.

Input format (typical):
n m max_t
beauty[0] beauty[1] ... beauty[n-1]
then m lines:
u v t

Output:
A single integer: the maximum total beauty achievable.

Notes:
- Roads are undirected (bidirectional).
- Total time is the sum of travel times of all roads used in your route.
- Beauty is counted once per distinct sight visited.
